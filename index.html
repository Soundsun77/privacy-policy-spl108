Privacy Policy for Sound Doctor JUN

Last updated: January 2025

Sound Doctor JUN is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our sound level measurement application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device Information

  • AdMob Advertising Data: Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and advertising-related information to display relevant advertisements.
  • App Usage Data: We collect calibration settings, measurement preferences, and app configuration data to improve your experience.

1.2 Permissions We Request

  • Microphone Access: Required for sound level measurement functionality.
  • Storage Access: Required for saving screenshots (Android 9 and below).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide sound level measurement services
  • To display relevant advertisements through AdMob
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To maintain app settings and calibration data

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.
  • AdMob: Google AdMob may collect advertising-related data as per their privacy policy.
  • Local Processing: All measurement data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.
  • No Audio Storage: We do not store or transmit any audio recordings.

4. Data Security

  • All measurement data is stored locally on your device
  • No audio files are saved or transmitted
  • Secure HTTPS connections are used for advertising services only
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information

5. Data Retention

  • App settings and calibration data are retained until you uninstall the app
  • AdMob data retention follows Google's privacy policy
  • You can clear app data through your device settings at any time

6. Your Rights

  • You can revoke microphone and storage permissions at any time
  • You can clear app data through device settings
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through AdMob settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@sounddoctorjun.com
  • Website: https://sounddoctorjun.com
  • Developer: Dr. JUN

10. Third-Party Services

10.1 Google AdMob

Our app uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob's privacy policy can be found at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

10.2 Google Play Services

Our app integrates with Google Play Services. Google's privacy policy can be found at: https://policies.google.com/privacy
